Section 01第一章

Brand Essence品牌本质

VERTU exists at the intersection of master artisanship and technological innovation. Founded in 1998 by Frank Nuovo within Nokia, the brand pioneered luxury mobile telephony — transforming communication devices into personal masterpieces crafted from the world's rarest materials.

VERTU 矗立于精湛工艺与前沿科技的交汇之处。品牌由 Frank Nuovo 于1998年在诺基亚内部创立，开创了奢华移动通讯的先河 — 将通信设备转化为以世界上最珍贵稀有材料打造的个人杰作。

Mission使命

To craft personal technology of uncompromising quality and enduring beauty — devices that are not merely tools, but expressions of individuality, status, and taste.

打造品质卓越、隽永之美的个人科技 — 不仅是工具，更是个性、地位与品味的表达。

Vision愿景

To be the definitive luxury technology house — where handcrafted artistry, rare materials, and intelligent innovation converge to serve those who shape the world.

成为引领世界的奢华科技品牌 — 手工艺术、珍稀材料与智能创新在此交融，服务于塑造世界的精英人群。

Brand Values品牌价值
Craftsmanship匠心工艺 Exclusivity至臻独享 Heritage传承底蕴 Innovation科技创新 Personalisation私人定制
Positioning品牌定位

VERTU occupies a singular position as the world's original luxury mobile phone brand. Competitors offer premium specs; VERTU offers handcrafted permanence. Each device is assembled by artisans in Naples and Ubrique across 320+ steps — using titanium, sapphire, gold, and exotic leathers from the same ateliers supplying the world's finest fashion houses.

VERTU 占据独特地位，作为世界上最早的奢华手机品牌。竞争对手提供高端配置，而 VERTU 提供手工打造的恒久价值。每台设备由那不勒斯和乌夫里克的工匠经320余道工序组装而成 — 使用钛金属、蓝宝石、黄金和来自为世界顶级时装品牌供货的同一工坊的珍稀皮革。

Section 02第二章

Logo System标志系统

The VERTU wordmark is the primary brand identifier. Set in custom uppercase letterforms, it communicates precision, authority, and permanence. It must never be modified, stretched, or recoloured outside the approved palette.

VERTU 文字标志是品牌的首要识别符。采用定制大写字母形态，传递精准、权威与永恒。标志不得以任何方式修改、拉伸或在核准色板之外重新着色。

Clear Space & Minimum Size安全空间与最小尺寸

Section 06第六章

Photography & Imagery摄影与图像

VERTU imagery should evoke tactility, material warmth, and quiet drama. Every photograph should feel as though you could reach in and touch the leather, feel the weight of titanium, sense the cool of sapphire crystal. Hover images below to see direction labels.

VERTU 图像应唤起触感、材质温度与静谧的戏剧性。每一张照片都应让人仿佛能伸手触摸皮革、感受钛金属的重量、体会蓝宝石水晶的清凉。悬停图片可查看方向标注。

Product Photography产品摄影

Product imagery uses dark, low-key lighting with controlled specular highlights. Backgrounds are deep black (#080808) or dark gradients. The product commands attention through its own material presence — lighting should be dramatic but never theatrical.

产品图像采用暗调、低调照明与可控的镜面高光。背景为深黑色（#080808）或暗色渐变。产品通过自身材质的存在感吸引注意力 — 照明应具戏剧性但绝不夸张。

Principles核心原则
TactileLow-keyMaterial-firstWarm highlightsQuiet drama

Avoid: clinical white backgrounds, over-retouched skin, harsh direct flash, visible competitor branding, cluttered compositions. Every frame should breathe — generous negative space is a VERTU hallmark.

避免：临床感白色背景、过度修图的皮肤、刺目的直闪、可见的竞品标识、杂乱的构图。每一帧画面都应留有呼吸空间 — 充裕的负空间是 VERTU 的标志性特征。

VERTU 以传承的自信与现代智慧的清晰发声。语调从不喧哗、从不炫耀 — 它沉稳、笃定且引人入胜。我们不做推销，我们邀请探索。

Easing Curve缓动曲线

Primary: cubic-bezier(0.0, 0.0, 0.2, 1) — a smooth ease-out that decelerates gracefully, like a precision hinge coming to rest. Avoid linear or bouncy easing; they feel cheap and mechanical.

主缓动曲线：cubic-bezier(0.0, 0.0, 0.2, 1) — 平滑的缓出效果，如精密铰链般优雅减速。避免线性或弹跳缓动，它们显得廉价且机械。

Reduced Motion减弱动效

All animations must respect prefers-reduced-motion: reduce. Disable decorative motion, reduce transitions to instantaneous, ensure no content hides behind animation-gated reveals. This is a WCAG 2.2 requirement and a non-negotiable brand standard.

所有动画必须遵循 prefers-reduced-motion: reduce。禁用装饰性动效，将过渡缩短至瞬时完成，确保无内容隐藏在动画触发的显示效果之后。这是 WCAG 2.2 要求，也是不可妥协的品牌标准。

VERTU's spatial system is built on an 8px base unit. Generous whitespace is a hallmark of luxury — it signals care, intentionality, and editorial restraint. Density is the enemy of refinement.

VERTU 的空间系统基于 8px 基础单位构建。充裕的留白是奢华的标志 — 它传达用心、意图与编辑克制。密度是精致的敌人。

Grid网格系统

Digital layouts use a 12-column grid with 24px gutters on a 1320px max container. Mobile collapses to a single column with 20px edge margins. Key principle: content should never touch the edges of its container — always maintain at least 1 column of breathing room on each side at desktop.

数字布局采用12列网格，24px 列间距，最大容器宽度 1320px。移动端折叠为单列，边缘留白 20px。核心原则：内容绝不应触及容器边缘 — 桌面端两侧始终保持至少1列的呼吸空间。

Section 12第十二章

Usage Guidelines使用规范

The VERTU brand is a living system — powerful when applied with precision, fragile when handled carelessly. These rules protect the brand's integrity across all touchpoints.

VERTU 品牌是一个活的系统 — 精准运用则强大有力，草率对待则脆弱不堪。以下规则保障品牌在所有触点的完整性。

Logo标志
Do正确

Use only approved colour combinations: white/cream on dark, black on light, gold on charcoal. Maintain clear space.

仅使用核准的色彩组合：深底配白/奶白，浅底配黑，炭色底配金。保持安全空间。

Don't避免

Apply drop shadows, glows, gradients, or effects. Never stretch, rotate, crop, or recolour the wordmark.

添加阴影、发光、渐变或特效。绝不拉伸、旋转、裁切或重新着色文字标志。

Colour色彩
Do正确

VERTU Black dominates. Gold accents selectively — headlines, labels, CTAs. Generous negative space lets the palette breathe.

VERTU 黑色主导。金色仅点缀性使用 — 标题、标签、行动按钮。充裕的负空间让色彩呼吸。

Don't避免

Flood layouts with gold. Never use bright white (#FFF) as primary background — use Cream or Ivory. No neon or saturated accent colours.

布局中金色泛滥。绝不以亮白色（#FFF）作为主要背景 — 使用奶油色或象牙色。禁止霓虹或高饱和点缀色。

Typography字体排版
Do正确

Line-height 1.6–1.85 for body. Letter-spacing 0.15–0.3em for uppercase labels. Playfair Display for headlines, DM Sans for body.

正文行高 1.6–1.85。大写标签字间距 0.15–0.3em。标题使用 Playfair Display，正文使用 DM Sans。

Don't避免

Use weights below 400 on dark backgrounds. No decorative or script typefaces. No walls of uppercase body copy.

深色背景上使用低于 400 的字重。禁止装饰性或手写字体。禁止大面积大写正文。

Imagery图像
Do正确

Lead with material texture and product detail. Dark, atmospheric lighting. Generous negative space. Evoke touch and weight.

以材质纹理和产品细节为主导。暗调、氛围感照明。充裕的负空间。唤起触感与分量感。

Don't避免

Generic stock imagery. Bright flat lighting. Competitor products visible. Text overlaid on complex areas without gradient scrim.

通用素材图片。明亮平淡的照明。可见的竞品产品。未加渐变遮罩直接在复杂区域叠加文字。

Art Direction — Do's & Don'ts美术指导 — 规范示例
Do正确

Dark backgrounds (#080808). Controlled specular highlights. Material texture visible. Generous negative space. Product centered or placed on editorial grid intersections.

使用深色背景（#080808）。控制镜面高光。确保材质纹理清晰可见。保留充足留白。产品居中或放置于编辑网格交叉点。

Don't避免

White or bright backgrounds. Oversaturation or heavy filters. Over-cropping that hides product form. Product floating without grounding shadow. Cluttered lifestyle scenes that distract.

白色或明亮背景。过饱和或厚重滤镜。过度裁切导致产品形态丢失。产品悬浮缺少接地阴影。杂乱的生活场景干扰视觉焦点。

Colour Treatment色彩处理

All product imagery must maintain consistent colour grading: slightly warm shadows (never blue/cool), highlights that are cream-white not pure white, and mid-tones that preserve the natural colour of materials — gold must read as gold, not yellow. Apply a subtle grain overlay (0.5–2% noise) for tactile quality.

所有产品图片须保持一致的色彩分级：暗部略偏暖（绝不偏蓝/冷），高光呈奶白而非纯白，中间调保留材料本色 — 金色必须呈现为金色而非黄色。添加细微颗粒叠加（0.5–2% 噪点）以增强触感。

CSS — Scroll Reveal AnimationCSS — 滚动显现动画
CSS + JavaScript
/* CSS — Scroll Reveal */
.reveal {
  opacity: 0;
  transform: translateY(32px);
  transition: opacity 0.65s var(--ease-luxury),
              transform 0.65s var(--ease-luxury);
}
.reveal.visible {
  opacity: 1;
  transform: translateY(0);
}

/* Stagger children: add .reveal-d1 through .reveal-d5 */
.reveal-d1 { transition-delay: 0.1s; }
.reveal-d2 { transition-delay: 0.2s; }
.reveal-d3 { transition-delay: 0.3s; }

/* Respect user preference */
@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce) {
  .reveal { opacity: 1; transform: none; transition: none; }
}

// JavaScript — IntersectionObserver
const observer = new IntersectionObserver(
  (entries) => {
    entries.forEach(entry => {
      if (entry.isIntersecting) {
        entry.target.classList.add('visible');
        observer.unobserve(entry.target);
      }
    });
  },
  { threshold: 0.1, rootMargin: '0px 0px -30px 0px' }
);

document.querySelectorAll('.reveal')
  .forEach(el => observer.observe(el));
