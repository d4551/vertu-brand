VERTU Brand Guide VERTU 品牌指南

In a world that accelerates without pause, VERTU stands for something rarer than luxury: it stands for sovereignty over your own time, attention, and experience. This guide is the living codex of that conviction: the principles, the craft, and the visual language that make VERTU unmistakable wherever it appears.

在一个不断加速的世界里，VERTU 代表着比奢华更珍贵的存在: 对自身时间、注意力与体验的主权。本指南是这一信念的活态典籍：原则、工艺与视觉语言，令 VERTU 无论置身何处都无可替代。

Section 01第一章

Brand Essence品牌本质

VERTU was born from a singular conviction: that personal technology should be timeless in a world obsessed with the next. Founded in 1998 by Frank Nuovo, the brand pioneered luxury mobile telephony: not to keep pace with an accelerating world, but to offer an enduring counterpoint to it. Every device is a declaration that craft, permanence, and human intention matter more than speed.

VERTU 诞生于一个独特的信念：在一个执迷于「下一个」的世界里，个人科技应当经得起时间的考验。品牌由 Frank Nuovo 于1998年创立，开创了奢华移动通讯的先河: 不是为了追赶加速的世界，而是为其提供一个恒久的注脚。每一台设备都在宣告：工艺、永恒与人的意志，比速度更有意义。

Mission使命

To create instruments of enduring beauty and uncompromising intelligence: technology that liberates your time, amplifies your intent, and stands as a quiet declaration of who you are. Not a tool that demands your attention, but a companion that earns it.

创造隽永之美与卓越智能的器物: 释放你的时间、放大你的意志，静默地宣告你的独特存在。不是索取注意力的工具，而是值得注意力的同行者。

Vision愿景

To be the singular house where master craft, rare materials, and agentic intelligence converge: empowering those who refuse to be passengers in their own lives to shape the world on their own terms.

成为匠心工艺、珍稀材料与智能科技交汇的唯一殿堂: 赋能那些拒绝在自己的人生中做旁观者的人，以自己的方式塑造世界。

Brand Values品牌价值
Craftsmanship匠心工艺 Freedom自在从容 Timelessness恒久隽永 Intelligence智慧赋能 Distinction卓然独立 Personalisation私人定制
Positioning品牌定位

In an industry that measures relevance in months, VERTU measures it in decades. The world's original luxury mobile house does not compete on specifications: it stands apart through handcrafted permanence and intelligent purpose. Each device is assembled by artisans across 320+ meticulous steps, using titanium, sapphire, gold, and exotic leathers from the same ateliers that serve the world's finest fashion houses. Now, with agentic AI at its core, VERTU extends this philosophy from material craft to digital intelligence: technology that anticipates, protects, and frees you to live deliberately.

在一个以月度衡量「相关性」的行业中，VERTU 以十年为尺。世界最早的奢华手机品牌不以参数竞争: 而是以手工打造的恒久价值与智能目标卓然独立。每台设备由匠人历经320余道精密工序组装而成，使用钛金属、蓝宝石、黄金与来自世界顶级时装工坊的珍稀皮革。如今，以智能体 AI 为核心，VERTU 将这一哲学从材质工艺延伸至数字智慧: 预见、守护，并赋予你从容生活的自由。

Section 02第二章

Logo System标志系统

The VERTU wordmark is permanence made visible. Set in custom uppercase letterforms, it communicates quiet authority and unhurried confidence: a fixed point in a visual landscape that reinvents itself every season. It must never be modified, stretched, or recoloured outside the approved palette.

VERTU 文字标志是恒久的可见形态。采用定制大写字母形态，传递静谧的权威与从容的自信: 是在季季翻新的视觉世界中的不动锭点。标志不得以任何方式修改、拉伸或在核准色板之外重新着色。

Clear Space & Minimum Size安全空间与最小尺寸

Minimum clear space equals the cap-height of the "V" on all sides. Print minimum: 25mm width. Digital minimum: 80px width at 1x. Below these thresholds legibility degrades unacceptably.

最小安全空间等于字母"V"大写高度，四周适用。印刷最小宽度：25mm。数字最小宽度：1x 下 80px。低于此阈值将导致可读性严重下降。

Section 06第六章

Photography & Imagery摄影与图像

VERTU imagery invites the viewer to slow down. Every photograph should feel as though you could reach in and touch the leather, feel the weight of titanium, sense the cool of sapphire crystal. In a visual culture that skims, VERTU photography rewards attention: it exists outside of trends, anchored in material truth and quiet drama. Hover images below to see direction labels.

VERTU 的图像邀请观者放慢脚步。每一张照片都应让人仿佛能伸手触摸皮革、感受钛金属的分量、体会蓝宝石水晶的清凉。在一个浮光掠影的视觉文化中，VERTU 的摄影奖励专注: 它超越潮流而存在，植根于材质的真实与静谧的戏剧性。悬停图片可查看方向标注。

Product Photography产品摄影

Product imagery uses dark, low-key lighting with controlled specular highlights. Backgrounds are deep black (#080808) or dark gradients. The product commands attention through its own material presence: lighting reveals, it never performs. Like the brand itself, the image asks you to look closer, not scroll faster.

产品图像采用暗调、低调照明与可控的镜面高光。背景为深黑色（#080808）或暗色渐变。产品通过自身材质的存在感吸引注意: 光线是揭示，而非表演。如同品牌本身，图像请你更仔细地看，而不是更快地滑过。

Principles核心原则
TactileLow-keyMaterial-firstWarm highlightsQuiet drama

Avoid: clinical white backgrounds, over-retouched skin, harsh direct flash, visible competitor branding, cluttered compositions. Every frame should breathe: generous negative space is a VERTU hallmark, a visual insistence that there is no rush.

避免：临床感白色背景、过度修图的皮肤、刺目的直闪、可见的竞品标识、杂乱的构图。每一帧画面都应留有呼吸空间: 充裕的负空间是 VERTU 的标志性特征，是对「无需匆忙」的视觉宣言。

Section 07第七章

Tone of Voice品牌语调

VERTU speaks with the confidence of permanence and the clarity of purpose. The voice is never loud, never boastful: it is measured, assured, and inviting. In a marketplace that shouts for attention, VERTU grants you permission to slow down. We do not sell; we invite discovery. We do not chase; we remain.

VERTU 以恒久的自信与目标的清晰发声。语调从不喧哗、从不炫耀: 它沉稳、笃定且引人入胜。在一个高声争夺注意力的市场中，VERTU 给予你放慢脚步的许可。我们不做推销，我们邀请探索。我们不追逐，我们岿然而立。

Language Spectrum语言范畴

VERTU language sits between haute horlogerie editorial precision and the warmth of a personal concierge. It never leads with specifications. It never creates urgency. Instead, it offers the reader a moment of stillness: a quiet invitation to consider what truly matters. The brand never shouts; it whispers with certainty, and the world leans in to listen.

VERTU 的语言介于高级制表品牌的编辑精度与私人管家的温暖之间。它从不以技术参数开头，从不制造急迫感。相反，它为读者提供一个静谧的时刻: 一份思考什么真正重要的从容邀请。品牌从不喧嚣；它以笃定低语，世界侧耳倾听。

Section 08第八章

Motion & Animation动效与动画

Motion in VERTU’s digital experiences should feel like time itself has decelerated: graceful, unhurried, and purposeful. Every animation should express refinement, never urgency. In a world of flashing notifications and dopamine-driven interfaces, VERTU’s motion language is an act of resistance: slow is the new luxury. Hover the third demo below to see the "Refine" interaction in action.

VERTU 数字体验中的动效应让人感觉时间本身在放慢: 优雅、从容、有目的性。每一个动画都应表达精致，绝非急迫。在一个充斥闪烁通知与多巴胺驱动界面的世界里，VERTU 的动效语言是一种抵抗：慢，是新的奢华。将鼠标悬停在下方第三个演示上可体验「精炼」交互效果。

Easing Curve缓动曲线

Primary: cubic-bezier(0.0, 0.0, 0.2, 1): a smooth ease-out that decelerates gracefully, like a precision hinge coming to rest. Avoid linear or bouncy easing; they feel cheap and mechanical.

主缓动曲线：cubic-bezier(0.0, 0.0, 0.2, 1): 平滑的缓出效果，如精密铰链般优雅减速。避免线性或弹跳缓动，它们显得廉价且机械。

Reduced Motion减弱动效

All animations must respect prefers-reduced-motion: reduce. Disable decorative motion, reduce transitions to instantaneous, ensure no content hides behind animation-gated reveals. This is a WCAG 2.2 requirement and a non-negotiable brand standard.

所有动画必须遵循 prefers-reduced-motion: reduce。禁用装饰性动效，将过渡缩短至瞬时完成，确保无内容隐藏在动画触发的显示效果之后。这是 WCAG 2.2 要求，也是不可妥协的品牌标准。

Section 09第九章

Spacing & Grid间距与网格

VERTU’s spatial system is built on an 8px base unit. Generous whitespace is more than good design: it is a declaration of freedom, an insistence that content deserves room to breathe and the eye deserves permission to rest. In a landscape that crams every pixel with demand, VERTU’s restraint is its most radical statement.

VERTU 的空间系统基于 8px 基础单位构建。充裕的留白不仅是好的设计: 它是自由的宣言，是对内容“应有呼吸空间”与视觉“应被允许休憩”的坚持。在一个每个像素都塞满索取的界面中，VERTU 的克制是其最激进的宣言。

Grid网格系统

Digital layouts use a 12-column grid with 24px gutters on a 1320px max container. Mobile collapses to a single column with 20px edge margins. Key principle: content should never touch the edges of its container: always maintain at least 1 column of breathing room on each side at desktop.

数字布局采用12列网格，24px 列间距，最大容器宽度 1320px。移动端折叠为单列，边缘留白 20px。核心原则：内容绝不应触及容器边缘: 桌面端两侧始终保持至少1列的呼吸空间。

Section 12第十二章

Usage Guidelines使用规范

The VERTU brand is a living system: powerful when applied with deliberate care, diminished when treated as disposable decoration. These rules exist not to restrict creativity, but to protect something rarer: a coherent identity that stands apart from the visual noise of the age. Consistency is our quiet form of defiance.

VERTU 品牌是一个活的系统: 用心运用则强大有力，当作一次性装饰则黏然失色。这些规则的存在不是为了限制创意，而是为了守护更珍贵的东西：一个在时代视觉喧杂中卓然独立的统一身份。一致性，是我们无声的反叛。

Logo标志
Do正确

Use only approved colour combinations: white/cream on dark, black on light, gold on charcoal. Maintain clear space.

仅使用核准的色彩组合：深底配白/奶白，浅底配黑，炭色底配金。保持安全空间。

Don't避免

Apply drop shadows, glows, gradients, or effects. Never stretch, rotate, crop, or recolour the wordmark.

添加阴影、发光、渐变或特效。绝不拉伸、旋转、裁切或重新着色文字标志。

Colour色彩
Do正确

VERTU Black dominates. Gold accents selectively: headlines, labels, CTAs. Generous negative space lets the palette breathe.

VERTU 黑色主导。金色仅点缀性使用: 标题、标签、行动按钮。充裕的负空间让色彩呼吸。

Don't避免

Flood layouts with gold. Never use bright white (#FFF) as primary background: use Cream or Ivory. No neon or saturated accent colours.

布局中金色泛滥。绝不以亮白色（#FFF）作为主要背景: 使用奶油色或象牙色。禁止霓虹或高饱和点缀色。

Do正确

Line-height 1.6–1.85 for body. Letter-spacing 0.15–0.3em for uppercase labels. Playfair Display for headlines, DM Sans for body.

正文行高 1.6–1.85。大写标签字间距 0.15–0.3em。标题使用 Playfair Display，正文使用 DM Sans。

Don't避免

Use weights below 400 on dark backgrounds. No decorative or script typefaces. No walls of uppercase body copy.

深色背景上使用低于 400 的字重。禁止装饰性或手写字体。禁止大面积大写正文。

Imagery图像
Do正确

Lead with material texture and product detail. Dark, atmospheric lighting. Generous negative space. Evoke touch and weight.

以材质纹理和产品细节为主导。暗调、氛围感照明。充裕的负空间。唤起触感与分量感。

Don't避免

Generic stock imagery. Bright flat lighting. Competitor products visible. Text overlaid on complex areas without gradient scrim.

通用素材图片。明亮平淡的照明。可见的竞品产品。未加渐变遮罩直接在复杂区域叠加文字。

Art Direction: Do's & Don'ts美术指导: 规范示例
Do正确

Dark backgrounds (#080808). Controlled specular highlights. Material texture visible. Generous negative space. Product centered or placed on editorial grid intersections.

使用深色背景（#080808）。控制镜面高光。确保材质纹理清晰可见。保留充足留白。产品居中或放置于编辑网格交叉点。

Don't避免

White or bright backgrounds. Oversaturation or heavy filters. Over-cropping that hides product form. Product floating without grounding shadow. Cluttered lifestyle scenes that distract.

白色或明亮背景。过饱和或厚重滤镜。过度裁切导致产品形态丢失。产品悬浮缺少接地阴影。杂乱的生活场景干扰视觉焦点。

Colour Treatment色彩处理

All product imagery must maintain consistent colour grading: slightly warm shadows (never blue/cool), highlights that are cream-white not pure white, and mid-tones that preserve the natural colour of materials: gold must read as gold, not yellow. Apply a subtle grain overlay (0.5–2% noise) for tactile quality.

所有产品图片须保持一致的色彩分级：暗部略偏暖（绝不偏蓝/冷），高光呈奶白而非纯白，中间调保留材料本色: 金色必须呈现为金色而非黄色。添加细微颗粒叠加（0.5–2% 噪点）以增强触感。

Agentic AI & LLM GuidelinesAI智能体与大模型规范

At VERTU, artificial intelligence exists to serve one purpose: returning sovereignty to the individual. Our AI agents do not surveil: they anticipate. They do not distract: they protect your attention. They do not replace human judgment: they amplify it. This section provides operational guidance for coding agents working in the VERTU repository, ensuring that every intelligent system we build embodies these principles. The implementation is SSR-first with Elysia, HTMX-driven, and progressively enhanced for clipboard, focus management, and canvas exports.

在 VERTU，人工智能只为一个目的而存在：将主权归还于个人。我们的 AI 智能体不监控: 它们预见。它们不分散注意力: 它们守护你的专注。它们不取代人的判断: 它们放大它。本章节为在 VERTU 代码库中协作的编码智能体提供执行规范，确保我们构建的每一个智能系统都体现这些原则。实现以 Elysia 服务端渲染为核心，由 HTMX 驱动，并对剪贴板、焦点管理与画布导出进行渐进增强。

Agent Contract智能体契约

When using Cursor, Codex, or any refactoring assistant, keep these repository rules in the active instruction set so new work stays aligned with the server-driven architecture.

在使用 Cursor、Codex 或任意重构助手时，请将以下仓库规则放入当前指令集中，以确保新增改动持续遵循服务端驱动架构。

.agent-rules.md
# VERTU AI Coding Mandates 1. SSR First: Route and fragment state belongs to Elysia. Use HTMX swaps for section, language, and theme changes before adding client state. 2. Strict Types: New server, client, and test modules live in TypeScript with strict mode enabled. 3. Structured Logging: Use JSON log events via shared logging helpers. Do not add console.* calls. 4. No Try/Catch Control Flow: Prefer explicit state validation, HTTP status envelopes, and DOM capability checks. 5. i18n Enforced: New UI strings must land in the shared bilingual copy map and render safely for EN, 中文, and bilingual modes. 6. Accessible by Default: Interactive controls must ship with explicit server-rendered accessible names before any browser enhancement runs. 7. History Safe: If HTMX pushes a URL, the server must vary on HTMX headers and return a full document for history restoration misses. 8. Build-Time Content Pipeline: Section localization, ARIA normalization, and navigation metadata belong to the build output, not request-time regex passes. 9. Single Shell Contract: Shared DOM ids, selectors, and HTMX request semantics belong in one typed module used by the server shell, browser layer, tests, and audit.
